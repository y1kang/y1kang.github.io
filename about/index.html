AboutBlogs

I am a Ph.D. student at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, where I have the fortune of being advised by Prof. Sze Yiu Chau. Before coming to CUHK, I obtained my Bachelor’s degree in Information Security from Jinan University.

My research interests are focused on precise program analysis to enhance software security, particularly in modeling security vulnerabilities as program analysis problems. I believe that the more the (good/bad) program patterns are understood, the safer the design becomes.
